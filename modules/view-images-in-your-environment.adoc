[id="view-images-in-your-environment_{context}"]
= Viewing images in your environment

[role="_abstract"]
With {product-title} you can identify and analyze all container images in your environment.

.Procedure
. Navigate to the RHACS portal and click *Vulnerability Management* from the left-hand navigation menu.
. To view details for all the images in your cluster, on the *Vulnerability Management* view header, select *Images*.
